World's Highest Volcanoes

There are many different ways to define 'tallest' or 'highest', depending on what the starting point of the measurement is. However, for our purposes today, we're talking about the three volcanoes that reach the highest elevation. All three are part of the Andes Mountain Range.

  1. Ojos del Salado, at 6,893 Meters in height.
  2. Monte Pissis, at 6,793 Meters in height.
  3. Nevado Tres Cruces, at 6,748 Meters in height.
